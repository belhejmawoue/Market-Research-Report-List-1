<p><p>The construction insulation materials market encompasses various types, each with unique properties. Glass wool is lightweight and effective in thermal and acoustic insulation. Rockwool, or stonewool, offers fire resistance and moisture control. Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) is a cost-effective choice with good thermal resistance, while Extruded Polystyrene (XPS) provides enhanced moisture resistance. Polyurethane foams deliver excellent thermal performance and versatility. Polyisocyanurate is known for its high insulation value, while phenolic foams offer low thermal conductivity. Other materials may include cellulose and denim insulation.</p></p>
