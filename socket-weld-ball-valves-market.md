<p><p>Socket weld ball valves are categorized into three main types: 1-piece, 2-piece, and 3-piece. </p><p>1-piece ball valves are integrated into a single body, offering a compact design and lower leakage potential. </p><p>2-piece valves consist of two main components, allowing for easier maintenance and repair, while maintaining robust performance. </p><p>3-piece valves have a three-part construction, enabling enhanced accessibility for servicing and better alignment during installation. Each type caters to specific industrial applications, balancing factors like cost, space, and serviceability.</p></p>
