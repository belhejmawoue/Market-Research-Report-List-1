<p><p>The Stainless Steel Horizontal Multistage Centrifugal Pump market is segmented into self-priming and non-self-priming types. Self-priming pumps are designed to draw fluid into the pump on their own, making them ideal for applications with varying liquid levels or when the pump is positioned above the fluid source. Non-self-priming pumps, on the other hand, require priming prior to operation and are typically used in stable environments where the liquid source is accessible. Both types are known for durability and efficiency.</p></p>
