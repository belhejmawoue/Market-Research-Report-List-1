<p><p>Propeller shaft couplings are essential components in marine and automotive applications, designed to connect drive shafts while accommodating misalignment and transmitting torque. Solid hub couplings feature a single, robust design that offers high torque capacity and reliability. Split hub couplings provide ease of maintenance by allowing quick disassembly without removing the entire assembly. Tapered couplings utilize a conical shape to ensure a secure fit and distribute loads evenly, enhancing performance and reducing wear. Each type serves unique operational needs and installation preferences.</p></p>
