<p><p>The Electric Vehicles Battery Management System (BMS) market can be categorized into three main types: distributed, centralized, and modular. Distributed BMS allows individual battery cells to be monitored independently, enhancing fault tolerance and efficiency. Centralized BMS consolidates cell monitoring into a single unit, simplifying installation and reducing costs. Modular BMS employs a scalable approach, enabling easy integration of additional modules as needed, making it ideal for varying vehicle sizes and battery configurations. Each type offers unique advantages for specific applications.</p></p>
