<p><p>The Tuberculosis Diagnosis and Treatment Market comprises various testing methods. Laboratory Testing involves traditional culture methods to identify tuberculosis pathogens. Nucleic Acid Tests (NAT) offer rapid detection of TB bacteria through genetic material analysis. The Mantoux Test measures skin reaction to TB antigens for latent infection screening. Radiography, particularly chest X-rays, helps visualize lung abnormalities indicative of active TB. Drug Susceptibility Tests determine the effectiveness of specific antibiotics against the strain, guiding appropriate treatment strategies for patients.</p></p>
