<p><p>The loading robots market comprises several key types, including articulated robots, Cartesian robots, and SCARA robots. Articulated robots, featuring rotational joints, excel in complex movements and handling irregularly shaped items. Cartesian robots operate on three linear axes, providing high precision and stability for loading tasks. SCARA robots, known for their selective compliance and rapid operation, are ideal for assembly and pick-and-place applications. Each type offers unique advantages tailored to specific industrial needs, enhancing efficiency and productivity in automated loading processes.</p></p>
