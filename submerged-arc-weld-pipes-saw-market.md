<p><p>Submerged Arc Weld (SAW) pipes are essential components in various industries due to their high strength and durability. The market is primarily divided into two types: Spiral Submerged Arc Welded (SSAW) pipes and Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welded (LSAW) pipes. SSAW pipes are produced by spirally welding steel sheets, making them suitable for transporting fluids over long distances. LSAW pipes, on the other hand, are made from flat steel plates welded along their length, offering superior mechanical properties and precision. Both types cater to diverse applications in infrastructure and energy sectors.</p></p>
