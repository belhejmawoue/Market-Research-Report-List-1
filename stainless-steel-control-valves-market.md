<p><p>The stainless steel control valves market includes several types designed for specific applications. Ball control valves offer quick, quarter-turn operation, ideal for on/off control. Butterfly control valves are lightweight and suitable for large flow rates, providing efficient regulation. Cryogenic control valves are engineered for extreme low temperatures, ensuring safe operation in cryogenic applications. Globe control valves are known for their excellent throttling capabilities and flow regulation. Other types may include pinch valves and diaphragm valves, catering to various niche requirements.</p></p>
