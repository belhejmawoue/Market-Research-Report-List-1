<p><ul><li>Transmitter ICs</li><li>Receiver ICs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless charging IC market is primarily divided into two types: transmitter ICs and receiver ICs. Transmitter ICs are responsible for generating an electromagnetic field to transfer energy from the charging station to the device. They manage power output and communication protocols. Conversely, receiver ICs are embedded in devices to capture the energy and convert it into usable power for charging the battery. Together, these components enable efficient wireless charging solutions for various applications, including smartphones and electric vehicles.</p></p>
