<p><p>Home infusion services encompass various types of delivery methods for therapeutic solutions. Intravenous therapy involves administering medications directly into the bloodstream through a vein, providing rapid therapeutic effects. Subcutaneous infusion delivers medication into the fatty tissue under the skin, allowing for slower absorption and extended release. Other methods may include intramuscular or oral routes, catering to specific patient needs and preferences. These diverse techniques facilitate effective treatment in the comfort of home, enhancing patient compliance and quality of life.</p></p>
