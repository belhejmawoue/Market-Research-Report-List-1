<p><p>The Lithium-Ion Battery CNT conductive agent market is divided into two types: single-walled carbon nanotubes (SWCNT) and multi-walled carbon nanotubes (MWCNT). SWCNTs consist of a single layer of carbon atoms arranged in a cylindrical structure, offering high electrical conductivity and mechanical strength, making them ideal for enhancing battery performance. In contrast, MWCNTs, composed of multiple concentric layers of carbon, provide excellent conductivity and greater surface area, beneficial for large-scale applications and cost-effectiveness in battery production.</p></p>
