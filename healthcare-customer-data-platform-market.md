<p><p>The Healthcare Customer Data Platform (CDP) market is classified into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise CDPs are installed and operated within an organization's local infrastructure, offering greater control over data security and customization, but also requiring significant maintenance and upfront investment. In contrast, cloud-based CDPs are hosted on remote servers, providing scalability, easier integration, and lower upfront costs, while enabling real-time data access and collaboration across various healthcare touchpoints. Both types aim to enhance patient engagement and streamline data management.</p></p>
