<p><p>LCP Soft Boards are categorized into three market types based on their layer configurations: Single Layer, Dual Layer, and Multi-layer boards. </p><p>Single Layer boards offer basic functionality with one layer of liquid crystal polymer, suitable for simple applications. Dual Layer boards enhance performance with two layers, allowing for increased routing density and improved signal integrity. Multi-layer boards, comprising three or more layers, provide advanced capabilities, supporting complex circuitry and high-frequency applications, making them ideal for high-end electronics and critical communication devices.</p></p>
