<p><strong>The Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-Demand RPO</li><li>Function-Based RPO</li><li>Full RPO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) services encompass various market types tailored to organizations' hiring needs. On-Demand RPO provides flexible, as-needed recruitment support for specific projects or peak hiring periods. Function-Based RPO focuses on outsourcing particular HR functions, such as sourcing or screening, while allowing other recruitment tasks to remain in-house. Full RPO entails a comprehensive takeover of an organization's entire recruitment process, delivering end-to-end solutions, including strategy, sourcing, candidate management, and onboarding, maximizing efficiency and effectiveness in hiring.</p></p>
