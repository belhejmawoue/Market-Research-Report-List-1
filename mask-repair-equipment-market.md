<p><p>The mask repair equipment market revolves around technologies such as Laser Technology, Focused Ion Beam (FIB) Technology, and Nanomachining Technology. Laser Technology utilizes high-intensity lasers to precisely ablate defects on photomasks. Focused Ion Beam (FIB) Technology employs a focused beam of ions to mill and modify mask surfaces at the nanoscale, enabling intricate repairs. Nanomachining Technology encompasses advanced techniques for manipulating materials at the atomic level, allowing for superior precision in mask correction, essential for high-resolution semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
