<p><strong>The Meniscus Implants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Allograft</li><li>Synthetic</li><li>Xenograft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The meniscus implants market consists of three main types: allograft, synthetic, and xenograft. Allograft implants utilize donated human tissue, offering biocompatibility and natural integration. Synthetic implants are made from artificial materials, designed for durability and to mimic meniscus function, but may lack biological integration. Xenograft implants are derived from animal tissues, providing an alternative to human tissues, though they carry a risk of rejection. Each type addresses specific needs in meniscus repair, catering to various patient requirements and surgical considerations.</p></p>
