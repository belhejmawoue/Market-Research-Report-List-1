<p><p>Non-linear TV services encompass different market types that provide flexible content delivery. Internet Protocol Television (IPTV) transmits television content via the internet, allowing for on-demand viewing, typically offered through telecom providers. Over-the-top Television (OTT) bypasses traditional distribution, delivering streaming content directly to viewers over the internet, often through subscription services. Both IPTV and OTT enable users to watch programs at their convenience, transforming traditional viewing habits and expanding access to diverse content offerings.</p></p>
