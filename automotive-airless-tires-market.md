<p><p>The automotive airless tires market features two main types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires have layers of fabric plies arranged at an angle, providing a sturdier structure and better load capacity, often used in commercial vehicles. In contrast, radial tires have plies arranged radially, allowing for greater flexibility and improved fuel efficiency, making them the preferred choice for passenger cars. Both types contribute uniquely to performance, safety, and durability, with airless designs aiming to enhance reliability and reduce maintenance.</p></p>
