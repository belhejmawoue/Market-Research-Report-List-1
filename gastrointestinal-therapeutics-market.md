<p><p>The gastrointestinal therapeutics market comprises two main types: oral and parenteral. Oral therapeutics involve medications administered through the mouth, providing convenience and ease of use for patients. These include tablets, capsules, and liquids that target conditions like acid reflux, ulcers, and irritable bowel syndrome. In contrast, parenteral therapeutics are delivered via injections or infusions, ensuring rapid absorption and effectiveness for patients unable to take medications orally. This route is often utilized for severe gastrointestinal disorders requiring immediate action.</p></p>
