<p><p>The smart irrigation controllers market is primarily divided into two types: weather-based controllers and sensor-based controllers. Weather-based controllers utilize real-time weather data to adjust irrigation schedules, optimizing water use based on rainfall and temperature. In contrast, sensor-based controllers rely on soil moisture sensors to determine when plants need water, ensuring precise application based on actual soil conditions. Both types aim to enhance water efficiency and promote sustainable agricultural practices while improving plant health and reducing costs.</p></p>
