<p><strong>The Wood-fired Hot Tub Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Heater</li><li>External Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood-fired hot tub market is primarily divided into two types based on heating mechanisms: internal heaters and external heaters. Internal heater models feature a wood-burning stove integrated directly inside the tub, providing efficient and direct heating. In contrast, external heater models position the stove outside the tub, using a heat exchange system to warm the water. Both types offer an eco-friendly alternative for outdoor relaxation, but the choice depends on user preferences for space, aesthetics, and heating efficiency.</p></p>
