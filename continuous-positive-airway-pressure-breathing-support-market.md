<p><p>The Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) breathing support market comprises two main types: Fixed Pressure CPAP devices and Auto Adjusting CPAP devices. Fixed Pressure CPAP devices deliver a constant prescribed air pressure throughout the night, ensuring a steady flow to keep airways open. In contrast, Auto Adjusting CPAP devices dynamically adjust the air pressure in real-time based on the user's needs, providing variable support for changing conditions, enhancing comfort and efficacy in managing sleep apnea and other respiratory issues.</p></p>
