<p><p>The sugar-derived surfactant market includes various types such as alkyl polyglycosides (APGs), decyl glucoside, and sucrose cocoate. APGs are non-ionic surfactants with excellent biodegradability and mildness, making them ideal for personal care and household products. Decyl glucoside, a gentle surfactant derived from corn glucose, is widely used in cosmetics for its skin-friendly properties. Sucrose cocoate, derived from sucrose and coconut fatty acids, offers emulsifying and moisturizing benefits, commonly used in body care formulations and hair products.</p></p>
