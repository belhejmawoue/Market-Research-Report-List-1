<p><ul><li>Dry Method</li><li>Wet Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PU slurry market can be segmented into two main types: dry method and wet method. The dry method involves mixing polyurethane materials in a controlled environment, resulting in a lower moisture content and improved particle uniformity, suitable for applications requiring precise formulations. Conversely, the wet method blends polyurethane with a liquid medium, providing enhanced flow properties and easier application, often utilized in coatings and adhesives. Both methods cater to diverse industrial needs based on the desired product characteristics and applications.</p></p>
