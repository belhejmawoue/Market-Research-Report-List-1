<p><p>The Emergency Cervical Collar market is primarily divided into two types: soft cervical collars and rigid cervical collars. Soft cervical collars provide support and mild immobilization, making them suitable for minor neck injuries or post-surgical recovery. They are often made of foam or fabric for comfort. Rigid cervical collars, on the other hand, offer stronger support and immobilization for severe injuries or trauma, typically constructed from hard plastic and designed to prevent any movement of the neck during emergency situations.</p></p>
