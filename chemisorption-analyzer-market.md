<p><strong>The Chemisorption analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dynamic Flow Chemisorption Analyzer</li><li>Static Chemisorption Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemisorption analyzer market is segmented into two main types: dynamic flow chemisorption analyzers and static chemisorption analyzers. Dynamic flow chemisorption analyzers continuously introduce gas into the sample chamber, allowing real-time measurement of adsorption properties under controlled flow conditions. In contrast, static chemisorption analyzers measure gas adsorption in a sealed environment, providing insights into the sample's surface characteristics without the influence of gas flow. Each type caters to specific research and industrial applications in material science and catalysis.</p></p>
