<p><p>The Contract Pharmaceutical Manufacturing market comprises several types: Oral, Injectable, API, and Others. Oral manufacturing involves producing solid dosage forms like tablets and capsules. Injectable manufacturing focuses on sterile solutions or suspensions administered via injections. API, or Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients, involves the production of the biologically active components used in medications. The "Other" category includes specialized forms like creams, ointments, and inhalable products, catering to diverse therapeutic requirements and advancing pharmaceutical innovation.</p></p>
